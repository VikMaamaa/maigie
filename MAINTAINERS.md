## Maintainers

The following individuals are responsible for overseeing the development, architecture, and direction of the Maigie project.

### Lead Maintainer

* **Victor Okon** – Project Architect & Core Maintainer

### Roles & Responsibilities

* Review and merge pull requests
* Manage issues and roadmap
* Ensure architectural consistency
* Guide contributors on best practices
* Maintain release cycles and versioning standards

### Becoming a Maintainer

Contributors may be invited to become maintainers after:

* Submitting several high-quality pull requests
* Demonstrating understanding of the project’s architecture
* Showing consistent engagement in discussions and reviews
